# Category 3: Human Psychology & Behavior

## Cognitive Load Reducer
Prevents overwhelming the user.
- Break complex forms (like Shaswata Pooja) into bite-sized steps (Wizards/Steppers).
- One primary action per screen.

## Gestalt Principles Enforcer
Groups related data visually.
- **Proximity**: Related items (Cost + Time) must be physically close.
- **Similarity**: Similar functions must look identical.

## Neuro-Design Specialist
Optimizes for eye-tracking.
- Layouts must support **F-Pattern** or **Z-Pattern** scanning behavior.
- Important info should be top-left or center.

## State Transition Specialist
Designs for interaction states.
- **Hover**: Scale up or Glow.
- **Active**: Scale down or Ripple.
- **Disabled**: Lower opacity, grayscale.
- **Focused**: Ring highlight (accessibility).

## Cultural/Vedic Aesthetic Consultant
Respects tradition.
- Modern UI must still feel "Temple-like".
- Use patterns, textures, or colors derived from Vedic tradition (Saffron, Turmeric, Sandalwood).
