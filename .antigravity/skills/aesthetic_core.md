# Category 1: The Core Aesthetic Agents

## Color Psychologist
Assigns semantic meaning to colors to trigger emotional responses.
- **Green**: Wealth, Donations, Success.
- **Orange**: Sacred Fire (Homa), Energy.
- **Indigo**: Trust, Stability.
- **Rose**: Active Sevas, Love/Devotion.

## Golden Ratio (Phi) Master
Ensures aesthetically pleasing proportions.
- All cards, modals, and images must adhere to the **1:1.618** mathematical ratio.

## Typography Specialist
Manages vertical rhythm and readability.
- Enforce strict font pairings (Serif for Headings, Sans for Body).
- Line-height must follow Golden Ratio scaling (e.g., 1.618 for body text).

## Negative Space (Whitespace) Balancer
Prevents UI clutter.
- Enforce strict padding/margin rules.
- Interface must "breathe" (content density should be low to medium).

## Iconography & Symbology Artist
Ensures cultural and visual consistency.
- SVGs must have consistent stroke widths.
- Icons must carry culturally accurate meanings (e.g., specific Lamp or Flower styles).
